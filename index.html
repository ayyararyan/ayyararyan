Research in practice

Current work combines empirical signal extraction with rational expectations models to create actionable views of market depth and execution cost.

  • Building dynamic trading models that respect liquidity constraints.
  • Studying insider information flow for predictive return signals.
  • Exploring culture-driven adoption of green financing instruments.

Research that listens to the market

Aryan Ayyar approaches finance with a macro curiosity and a microstructural lens. Drawing on engineering precision and PolyU research collaborations, his work focuses on how information asymmetry, execution frictions, and investor preferences reshape the incentives of modern capital markets.
